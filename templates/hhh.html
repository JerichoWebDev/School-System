About Us

Caraga State University (CSU), formerly Northern Mindanao State Institute of Science and Technology (NORMISIST), is a public university in the Philippines. It is mandated to provide advanced instruction in literature, philosophy, the sciences, and arts, and to undertake research and extension services, and provide progressive leadership in its areas of specialization.
